# FierceAI "Lite" Products Overview

This guide explains the "Lite" versions of FierceAI products, what they include, and how they differ from full versions.

---

## What Does "Lite" Mean?

"Lite" products in the FierceAI ecosystem are streamlined versions of tools designed for users who need essential features without the complexity or token costs of AI-powered content generation.

---

## AccelePosts Lite

### Quick Summary
Social media scheduling and posting tool **without** AI content generation features.

### What's Included
- ✅ **Platform Connections**: Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter), LinkedIn
- ✅ **Manual Post Creation**: Write and upload your own content
- ✅ **Visual Calendar**: Drag-and-drop scheduling interface
- ✅ **Multi-Platform Publishing**: Post to multiple platforms at once
- ✅ **Draft Management**: Save drafts for later
- ✅ **Basic Analytics**: Track engagement and performance

### What's NOT Included
- ❌ AI Text Generation for captions
- ❌ AI Image Generation with DALL-E 3
- ❌ AI Video Generation with Google Veo 2
- ❌ Campaign Management
- ❌ Advanced Analytics

### Best For
- Users with their own content ready
- Those who want to avoid token costs
- Simple social media scheduling needs
- Beginners learning social media management

### How to Upgrade
Start with AccelePosts Lite and upgrade to the full version at any time to access AI features. Token purchases required only for AI generation in full version.

---

## AcceleMail Lite

### Quick Summary
Email marketing platform with simplified features and AI content generation integrated into FierceAI.

### What's Included
- ✅ **Email Campaigns**: Create and send professional emails
- ✅ **AI Subject Lines**: Generate optimized subject lines with AI
- ✅ **AI Email Content**: Generate complete email body content with AI
- ✅ **AI Images**: Create custom images for emails
- ✅ **Contact Management**: Organize and manage email lists
- ✅ **Personalization**: Merge tags (first name, last name, etc.)
- ✅ **Analytics**: Track opens, clicks, and engagement
- ✅ **Scheduling**: Send immediately or schedule for later
- ✅ **Test Emails**: Preview before sending to your list

### What's NOT Included (vs. Enterprise Platforms)
- ❌ Complex automation workflows
- ❌ Advanced behavioral segmentation
- ❌ A/B testing
- ❌ Drip campaigns
- ❌ Lead scoring
- ❌ CRM integration
- ❌ Landing page builders

### Best For
- Small businesses starting with email marketing
- Content creators building audiences
- Users who want AI-powered email content
- Those who need simple, effective email campaigns

### Key Advantage
Unlike AccelePosts Lite, AcceleMail Lite **DOES include AI features** for content generation. It's "Lite" compared to enterprise email platforms, not compared to a "full" AcceleMail version.

## Common Questions

### "Why is AcceleMail called 'Lite' if it has AI features?"

AcceleMail Lite is "Lite" compared to enterprise email marketing platforms (like Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, etc.) that have complex automation, workflows, and CRM features. Within the FierceAI ecosystem, AcceleMail Lite is the complete email marketing solution with AI included.

### "Can I upgrade from Lite versions?"

**AccelePosts**: Yes, upgrade to full AccelePosts to get AI text, image, and video generation plus campaign management.

**AcceleMail**: AcceleMail Lite is the current email marketing solution. Future versions may include more advanced features.

### "Do Lite products cost less?"

Lite products are included in your FierceAI subscription based on your plan level. Full AccelePosts requires token purchases for AI features. AcceleMail Lite requires tokens for AI content generation.

### "Which should I use?"

**Use AccelePosts Lite if:**
- You already have social media content (graphics, videos, text)
- You just need scheduling and posting
- You want to minimize token costs

**Upgrade to Full AccelePosts if:**
- You want AI to create your content
- You need video generation
- You manage multiple campaigns
- You want advanced analytics

**Use AcceleMail Lite if:**
- You need email marketing
- You want AI to help write emails
- You send newsletters or promotions
- You track email engagement
