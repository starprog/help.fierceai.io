# Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about FierceAI.

## Account & Billing

### How do I upgrade my plan?
Navigate to your account settings and click "Billing & Plans". Select your desired plan and follow the payment process.

### What happens if I exceed my usage limits?
Your account will be temporarily limited until the next billing cycle. Upgrade your plan for higher limits.

### Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

## Content Creation

### How do I get better results from the AI?
- Be specific and detailed in your prompts
- Provide context and examples
- Specify the desired tone and style
- Use the iteration features to refine results

### Can I edit AI-generated content?
Absolutely! All generated content can be edited, refined, and customized to meet your specific needs.

### Is my content private?
Yes, your generated content is private and stored securely in your account.

## Technical Issues

### Why is content generation slow?
Generation speed depends on complexity and current server load. Try simplifying your request or try again during off-peak hours.

### My images aren't generating correctly
Check that your prompts are clear and specific. Avoid contradictory instructions and try different approaches.

### I can't access certain features
Ensure your plan includes the features you're trying to use. Some advanced features require higher-tier plans.
