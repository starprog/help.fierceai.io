## üé´ Submit a Support Ticket

**For Complex Issues:**

1. **Prepare Your Information**
   - Account email address
   - Description of the issue
   - Steps to reproduce the problem
   - Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)
   - Error messages (if any)

2. **Send to Support**
   - Email: support@fierceai.io
   - Subject: Include your issue category (e.g., "Billing Issue" or "Generation Error")
   - Include all relevant details

3. **Track Your Ticket**
   - You'll receive a ticket number
   - Response within 24 hours
   - Follow-up until resolution

## üí° Tips for Faster Support

**Provide Details**
- Describe the issue clearly
- Include your account email
- Attach relevant screenshots
- List steps you've already tried

**Be Specific**
- Which tool were you using?
- What were you trying to do?
- What happened vs. what you expected?
- When did the issue start?

**Check Status Page**
- Before reporting outages, check our status page
- See if there are known issues
- Get real-time updates on incidents
