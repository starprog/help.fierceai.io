# Quick Tutorial

Welcome! This tutorial will walk you through creating your first AI content with FierceAI.

## Step 1: Access the Dashboard

1. Log in to your FierceAI account
2. Navigate to the main dashboard
3. Familiarize yourself with the interface

## Step 2: Choose an AI Tool

For this tutorial, we'll use the Text Generation tool:

1. Click on **AI Tools** in the sidebar
2. Select **Text Generation**
3. Choose **Article Writer** or **Blog Post Creator**

## Step 3: Create Your First Generation

### Writing a Prompt
```text
Example prompt:
"Write a 500-word blog post about the benefits of AI in small businesses. 
Include specific examples and write in a professional but approachable tone 
for business owners."
```

### Key Elements:
- **Length**: Specify word count
- **Topic**: Be specific about the subject
- **Tone**: Mention the desired style
- **Audience**: Who will read this content

## Step 4: Generate and Review

1. **Click Generate** - Wait for the AI to create your content
2. **Review the Output** - Check quality and relevance
3. **Make Edits** - Customize as needed
4. **Save or Export** - Download or copy your content

## Step 5: Monitor Usage

- Check your token usage in the dashboard
- Review the generation history
- Note what prompts work best

## Tips for Success

### Better Prompts = Better Results
- Be specific about what you want
- Include context and background
- Specify format (lists, paragraphs, etc.)
- Mention target audience
