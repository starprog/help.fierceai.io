# Dashboard

## Overview

The Dashboard is your central command center in FierceAI. It provides a comprehensive view of your account, quick access to AI tools, usage statistics, and personalized recommendations to help you maximize productivity.

## Key Features

### 1. AI Assistant Search

At the top of your dashboard, you'll find a powerful search bar that allows you to:

- Quickly find and launch any AI tool or template
- Search through all available AI features
- Access tools without navigating through menus
- Start creating content immediately

**Quick Tip:** Use the search bar to discover new tools and features you might not have explored yet.

### 2. Filter Navigation

The dashboard includes filter tabs to help you organize your workspace:

- **All** - View all dashboard sections
- **AI Assistant** - Quick access to all AI tools
- **Your Plan** - Jump to subscription details
- **Team Members** - Manage team collaboration (if enabled)
- **Recent** - View recently created documents
- **Documents** - Access your document library
- **Templates** - Browse favorite templates
- **Overview** - Return to main dashboard view

## Dashboard Sections

### Your Plan & Credits

This section displays your current subscription status and remaining token balance:

#### What You'll See:

- **Current Plan Name** - Your active subscription tier
- **Renewal Date** - Days remaining until your next refill
- **Word Tokens** - Remaining tokens for text-based AI features
- **Image Tokens** - Remaining credits for image generation (if applicable)
- **Visual Credit Chart** - Interactive donut chart showing credit distribution across models

#### Quick Actions:

- **View Credits** - See detailed breakdown by AI model
- **Select a Plan** - Upgrade or change your subscription
- **Cancel My Plan** - Manage subscription cancellation

**For Team Plans:**
- View team workspace name and manager
- Check team's shared credit balance
- Contact team manager for additional credits

### Account Summary

Track your productivity and content creation at a glance:

#### Statistics Displayed:

- **Hours Saved** - Estimated time saved using AI (3 hours per document)
- **Total Documents** - Number of documents you've created
- **Chatbots** - Number of custom chatbots you've built

#### Document Overview:

Visual breakdown of your content by type:

- **Text Documents** (Blue) - Blog posts, articles, copy
- **Image Documents** (Green) - AI-generated images
- **Audio Documents** (Gray) - Voice and audio content
- **Other Documents** - Additional content types

Click **"View All"** to access your complete document library.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often do credits refill?**  
A: Credits automatically refill on your subscription renewal date, shown in the "Your Plan" section.

**Q: What does "Hours Saved" mean?**  
A: This estimates the time you've saved by using AI, calculated as 3 hours per document created.

**Q: Can I invite team members on all plans?**  
A: Team functionality is only available on Team plans. Upgrade to access this feature.

**Q: How do I access premium templates?**  
A: Premium templates require a Premium or All plan. Click any template marked "Upgrade" to see plan options.

**Q: Where can I find my created documents?**  
A: Click "View All" in the "Recently Launched" section or navigate to Documents from the main menu.
