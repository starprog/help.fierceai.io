### 2. Understand Your Credits

**How Credits Work:**
- **Word Credits**: Used for text generation (AI Writer, Chat, Rewriter, etc.)
- **Image Credits**: Used for image generation and editing
- **Specialized Credits**: Some tools may use specific credit types

**Monitor Usage:**
- Check your remaining credits in the dashboard header
- View detailed usage in [Usage Analytics](https://fierceai.io/dashboard/user/usage)
- Set up low-credit notifications

### 4. Create Your First Content

**Choose Your Path:**

**Option A: Use AI Writer**
1. Go to [AI Writer](https://fierceai.io/dashboard/user/openai/generator/ai-article-wizard-generator)
2. Select a template or start from scratch
3. Enter your topic or instructions
4. Click "Generate" and review results
5. Edit and refine as needed
6. Save to [My Documents](https://fierceai.io/dashboard/user/openai/documents/all)

**Option B: Generate Images**
1. Go to [AI Image Generator](https://fierceai.io/dashboard/user/openai/generator/ai-image-generator)
2. Describe the image you want
3. Choose style and settings
4. Click "Generate"
5. Download or save to Content Manager

**Option C: Chat with AI**
1. Go to [AI Chat](https://fierceai.io/dashboard/user/openai/chat)
2. Start a new conversation
3. Ask questions or request assistance
4. Save important responses
5. Continue conversations across sessions

### 5. Organize Your Work

**Document Management:**
- Save generated content to [My Documents](../content-creation/my-documents.md)
- Use folders to organize projects
- Add tags for easy searching
- Star favorites for quick access

**Content Manager:**
- Store files and media in [Content Manager](../content-creation/content-manager.md)
- Upload images, documents, and assets
- Access files across all AI tools
- Share with team members

### 6. Customize Your Experience

**Brand Voice:**
- Create your [Brand Voice](../ai-tools/brand-voice.md) profile
- Define tone, style, and vocabulary
- Apply to all AI-generated content
- Maintain consistent brand identity

**Templates:**
- Save frequently used prompts as templates
- Create custom workflows
- Share templates with team members
- Build your template library

### 7. Set Up Integrations

**Connect Your Platforms:**

**WordPress Integration:**

- [Learn more about WordPress integration](integrations.md#wordpress-integration)

**FierceFiles Integration:**
- Cloud storage for your files
- [Learn more about FierceFiles](integrations.md#fiercefiles-integration)

**FierceLinks Integration:**
- Shorten and track URLs
- [Learn more about FierceLinks](integrations.md#fiercelinks-integration)

### 8. Invite Team Members

**Collaborate with Your Team:**
- Invite team members from [Team Members](team-members.md)
- Assign roles and permissions
- Allocate credits per member
- Share workspaces and documents
