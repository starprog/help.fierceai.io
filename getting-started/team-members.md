## ‚ûï Inviting Team Members

### Invitation Process

1. **Click "Invite Member"** button
2. **Enter Email Address**: Type the team member's email
3. **Select Role**: Choose their permission level
4. **Assign Credits** (optional): Allocate word and image credits
5. **Send Invitation**: Email will be sent automatically

### Invitation Email

Invited members receive an email with:
- Welcome message from your team
- Link to join the team
- Instructions for accepting the invitation
- Team name and your contact information

### Member Status

**Waiting**
- Invitation sent but not yet accepted
- Member hasn't joined the team
- Can resend invitation if needed

**Active**
- Member has accepted invitation
- Full access to assigned features
- Can start using allocated credits

**Inactive**
- Member deactivated by team owner
- Cannot access team resources
- Can be reactivated at any time

## ‚úèÔ∏è Managing Team Members

### Editing Members

1. Click the **pencil icon** next to any member
2. Update the following settings:
   - **Role**: Change permission level
   - **Status**: Activate or deactivate member
   - **Credits**: Adjust allocated resources
3. Click **"Save"** to apply changes

### Editable Fields

**Role**
- Member: Basic access to tools
- Manager: Higher permissions, can manage others
- Custom roles (if configured)

**Status**
- Active: Full access enabled
- Inactive: Temporarily disable access
- Use for vacation, offboarding, or access control

**Credit Allocation**
- **Word Credits**: Number of AI-generated words available
- **Image Credits**: Number of AI-generated images available
- **Unlimited Credits**: Toggle to remove limits for this member
- Credits are taken from team owner's pool

### Deleting Members

1. Click the **X icon** next to the member
2. Confirm deletion (this is permanent)
3. ‚ö†Ô∏è **Warning**: This deletes all documents related to the user

**What Happens:**
- Member immediately loses access
- User's team_id is set to null
- All associated documents are deleted
- Action cannot be undone

## üé≠ Roles & Permissions

### Member Roles

**Member (Default)**
- Access to AI tools based on allocation
- Can create and manage own documents
- Cannot invite other members
- Limited administrative capabilities

**Manager**
- All Member permissions
- Can view team activity
- May assign tasks or projects
- Higher priority for resource allocation

**Owner (You)**
- Full administrative control
- Invite and remove members
- Manage all team settings
- Allocate credits and resources
- Cannot be removed from team

### Permission Levels

Different roles may have access to:
- **AI Tools**: Which generators they can use
- **Templates**: Access to custom templates
- **Documents**: View/edit shared content
- **Credits**: How many tokens they can consume
- **Settings**: Team configuration access

## üí∞ Credit Management

### Credit Allocation

**Team Credit Pool**
- Credits come from team owner's subscription
- Total available shown on team dashboard
- Distributed among team members as needed

**Member Credits**
- **Word Credits**: For text generation (articles, chat, etc.)
- **Image Credits**: For image generation and editing
- **Used Credits**: Track what has been consumed
- **Remaining Credits**: Available balance

### Unlimited Credits

**Enable Unlimited**
- Toggle "Allow Unlimited Credits" for a member
- They can use resources without limits
- Useful for managers or power users
- Still counts against team total if applicable

**When to Use:**
- Trusted team members
- Critical projects requiring flexibility
- Testing and development phases
- Executive or management roles

### Credit Tracking

Monitor usage for each member:
- **Used Image Credit**: Total images generated
- **Used Word Credit**: Total words generated
- **Remaining Images**: Credits left for images
- **Remaining Words**: Credits left for text

## üìß Invitation Management

### Sending Invitations

**Automatic Email**
- System automatically sends email when you invite

1. Locate the member in team list
2. Click **"Resend Invitation"** button
3. New email sent immediately
4. Original invitation link still valid

### Accepting Invitations

**For New Users:**
1. Click invitation link in email
2. Create FierceAI account
3. Complete registration
4. Automatically added to team

**For Existing Users:**
1. Click invitation link
2. Log in to existing account
3. Confirm joining the team
4. Gain immediate access

## üö® Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Cannot Invite Members**
- **Problem**: "You need a team plan" error
- **Solution**: Upgrade to a plan that includes team functionality
- **Check**: Your subscription supports team features

**Invitation Not Received**
- **Check spam folder**: Emails sometimes filtered
- **Verify email address**: Typos prevent delivery
- **Resend invitation**: Use resend feature
- **Contact support**: If issue persists

**Member Cannot Accept**
- **Expired invitation**: Resend fresh invitation
- **Already a member**: User might already be in team
- **Account issues**: Member should contact support
- **Link broken**: Copy/paste might have truncated URL

**Credit Errors**
- **Insufficient credits**: Team owner needs more credits
- **Allocation exceeded**: Reduce other members' allocations
- **Sync issues**: Contact support for manual sync
- **Plan limits**: Upgrade for higher credit limits

**Permission Problems**
- **Wrong role assigned**: Edit member and change role
- **Status inactive**: Activate the member
- **Cache issues**: Log out and log back in
- **Account restrictions**: Check user's account status

## üí° Use Cases

### Agency Model

**Scenario**: Digital marketing agency with multiple clients and team members

**Structure:**
- Owner: Agency principal/CEO
- Managers: Account managers (3-5 people)
- Members: Content creators, designers (10-20 people)
- Clients: View-only access for approvals

**Credit Strategy:**
- Unlimited for account managers
- Fixed allocation per project team
- Client usage tracked separately
- Weekly review and reallocation

### Corporate Team

**Scenario**: In-house marketing team at large company

**Structure:**
- Owner: Marketing director
- Managers: Team leads for content, social, email
- Members: Specialists and coordinators
- Guests: External contractors (temporary)

**Credit Strategy:**
- Department-based allocation
- Priority for time-sensitive campaigns
- Quarterly budget reviews
- Rollover unused credits monthly

### Startup Team

**Scenario**: Small startup with tight budget

**Structure:**
- Owner: Founder/CEO
- Managers: Co-founder, marketing lead
- Members: All other team members (equal access)

**Credit Strategy:**
- Everyone gets equal base allocation
- Pool for shared projects
- Weekly sync on usage
- Flexible reallocation as needed

### Educational Institution

**Scenario**: University department teaching AI content creation

**Structure:**
- Owner: Department head/Professor
- Managers: Teaching assistants
- Members: Students (dozens)
- Guests: Guest lecturers

**Credit Strategy:**
- Per-project allocations
- Extra credits for high performers
- Reset each semester
- Educational use policies
