### How to Request a Withdrawal

1. **Check Your Balance**: Ensure your approved earnings meet the minimum threshold

### Withdrawal Statuses

- **Pending**: Request submitted and awaiting admin review
- **Approved**: Withdrawal approved and payment being processed
- **Paid**: Funds successfully transferred to your account
- **Rejected**: Request rejected (insufficient balance or invalid details)

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### When will I receive my commission?
Commissions are credited to your account immediately after a successful purchase. However, they enter "pending" status for verification before being approved for withdrawal.

### How long does verification take?
Most commissions are approved within 7-14 days after the purchase. This allows time for refund windows to close.

### Can I refer myself?
No, self-referrals are not permitted and will be flagged as fraudulent activity.

### What happens if my referral requests a refund?
If your referral requests a refund within their refund window, any associated commission will be revoked.

### Is there a limit to how much I can earn?
No! There's no cap on your potential earnings. The more qualified referrals you bring, the more you earn.

### Can I track individual referrals?
Yes, your dashboard shows all conversions with timestamps. For privacy reasons, personal information of referrals is not displayed.

### How do I promote my link effectively?
- Create honest reviews and tutorials
- Highlight specific features that solved your problems
- Share real results and use cases
- Engage with your audience authentically
- Provide value first, promote second
