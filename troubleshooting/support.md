# Contact Support

Need help with FierceAI Hub? We're here to assist you! This page provides all the ways you can get support and the information we need to help you quickly.

## Support Channels

### 1. Live Chat (Fastest Response)
Available directly in your dashboard:
- Click the **Help** button in the top navigation
- Available during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM EST)
- Average response time: 2-5 minutes

### 2. Support Tickets
For detailed issues that require investigation:
- Email: support@fierceai.io
- Response time: Within 24 hours
- Include your account ID and detailed description

### 3. Knowledge Base
Search our comprehensive documentation:
- Browse common solutions in [Common Issues](common-issues.md)
- Check [Error Codes](error-codes.md) reference
- Review [Performance Tips](performance.md)

## Before Contacting Support

To help us assist you faster, please:

### Gather Basic Information
- Your account email address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Time when the issue occurred

### Document the Issue
1. **What were you trying to do?**
2. **What actually happened?**
3. **What error messages did you see?**
4. **Can you reproduce the issue?**

### Check These First
- [ ] Clear your browser cache and cookies
- [ ] Try in an incognito/private browsing window
- [ ] Check if you have remaining tokens
- [ ] Verify your internet connection is stable
