### How Token Consumption Works

Tokens are calculated based on the generated output:

```
Tokens Used = (Number of words generated) √ó (Feature rate)
```

**Example**: If AI Writer generates a 500-word blog post and your feature rate is 1.0, you'll be charged approximately 500 tokens.

## Creating Your First Content

### Step 1: Access AI Writer

1. Log in to your FierceAI dashboard
2. Navigate to **AI Tools** > **AI Writer**
3. Or click the **Writer** icon in the main toolbar

### Step 2: Choose Content Type

1. Select from **20+ content templates**:
   - Blog Posts & Articles
   - Product Descriptions
   - Marketing Copy
   - Email Templates
   - Social Media Posts
   - And more...
2. Or choose **Custom Content** for freeform generation

### Step 3: Write Your Prompt

1. Describe what you want to create
2. Include key details:
   - Topic and main points
   - Target audience
   - Desired tone/style
   - Specific requirements
3. The more specific your prompt, the better the results

### Step 4: Configure Settings

1. **Length**: Short (100-300), Medium (300-800), Long (800-2000), or Custom
2. **Tone**: Professional, Casual, Persuasive, Educational, Creative
3. **Language**: English and 50+ other languages
4. **Creativity Level**: Standard, High, or Maximum

### Step 5: Generate & Review

1. Click **Generate Content**
2. Wait 5-15 seconds for generation
3. Review the generated content
4. Use built-in editor to refine
5. Copy, export, or save to your content library

## Best Practices

### Writing Effective Prompts

1. **Be Specific**: Include details about topic, audience, and purpose
2. **Provide Context**: Background information helps AI understand your needs
3. **Define Scope**: Specify length, format, and key points to cover
4. **Set Clear Tone**: Indicate the writing style you want
5. **Include Examples**: If you have a specific format in mind, describe it

### Good Prompt Example

```
Write an 800-word blog post about remote work productivity tips for software 
developers. Include 5 actionable strategies with examples. Use a professional 
but friendly tone. Target junior to mid-level developers working from home.
```

### Poor Prompt Example

```
Write about productivity
```

### Optimizing Content Quality

1. **Use Appropriate Templates**: Pre-configured templates are optimized for specific content types
2. **Adjust Creativity**: Higher creativity for marketing, lower for factual content
3. **Provide Sufficient Detail**: More context in prompts = better results
4. **Iterate Intelligently**: Make small adjustments to prompts rather than complete rewrites
5. **Edit Post-Generation**: AI-generated content often needs human polish

### Managing Token Usage

1. **Start with Shorter Length**: Generate less, expand if needed
2. **Review Before Regenerating**: Edit minor issues manually
3. **Use Batch Operations**: Generate multiple similar items at once
4. **Monitor Your Balance**: Check tokens before starting large projects
5. **Choose Standard Quality**: Reserve premium settings for important content

## Troubleshooting

### Content Not What You Expected

**Possible Causes:**
- ‚ùå Prompt lacks sufficient detail
- ‚ùå Wrong tone or style selected
- ‚ùå Content type doesn't match your needs

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Add more specific details to your prompt
- ‚úÖ Try different tone settings
- ‚úÖ Use AI Editor to modify the generated content
- ‚úÖ Generate multiple versions and combine best parts

### Quality Issues

**Possible Causes:**
- ‚ùå Insufficient context in prompt
- ‚ùå Vague or generic instructions
- ‚ùå Content type mismatch

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Provide more background information
- ‚úÖ Use specific examples in your prompt
- ‚úÖ Select appropriate content template
- ‚úÖ Increase creativity setting if content feels too generic

### "You have no credits left" Error

**Solution:**
- Check token balance at **Dashboard ‚Üí Token Management**
- Purchase tokens for the writing model this tool uses
- Visit the **AI Store** for token packages

### Generation Takes Too Long

**Possible Causes:**
- ‚ùå Server load during peak hours
- ‚ùå Very long content requested
- ‚ùå Complex prompt with many requirements

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Try during off-peak hours
- ‚úÖ Break long content into multiple generations
- ‚úÖ Simplify your prompt
- ‚úÖ Check your internet connection

### Content Contains Errors or Inaccuracies

**Possible Causes:**
- ‚ùå AI model limitations with specialized topics
- ‚ùå Outdated information in training data

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Always fact-check generated content
- ‚úÖ Provide specific, accurate information in prompts
- ‚úÖ Use AI Writer as a starting point, not final authority
- ‚úÖ Edit and verify technical details manually
