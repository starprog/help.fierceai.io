The wizard remembers your progress and allows you to refine each step before moving forward, ensuring you get exactly the content you need.

## How It Works

### Step 1: Access AI Article Wizard

1. Navigate to **Dashboard ‚Üí AI Tools ‚Üí AI Article Wizard**
2. Or visit directly: `https://fierceai.io/dashboard/user/openai/articlewizard/new?ai_article_wizard`

### Step 2: Generate Keywords

Enter your article topic and let AI generate relevant keywords:

```
Topic: "Sustainable gardening practices"
Number of keywords: 5-10
Language: English
```

The AI will suggest keywords like:
- Composting
- Organic fertilizers
- Water conservation
- Native plants
- Permaculture

**You can:**
- Accept suggested keywords
- Generate more options
- Manually add/edit keywords
- Mix AI suggestions with your own

### Step 3: Create Article Title

Based on your keywords, generate engaging titles:

```
Maximum title length: 60 characters (for SEO)
Number of options: 5
```

Example titles generated:
- "10 Sustainable Gardening Practices for Eco-Friendly Gardens"
- "Beginner's Guide to Sustainable Gardening"
- "How to Create an Eco-Friendly Garden: Expert Tips"

**Select one or create your own custom title.**

### Step 4: Build Article Outline

Generate a structured outline for your article:

```
Number of sections: 5-8
Based on: Your keywords and title
```

Example outline:
1. Introduction to Sustainable Gardening
2. Composting Techniques
3. Water Conservation Methods
4. Choosing Native Plants
5. Organic Pest Control
6. Conclusion and Next Steps

**Customize the outline** by adding, removing, or reordering sections.

### Step 5: Select or Generate Images

Choose how to add visual content:

**Options:**
- **Pexels**: Free stock photos
- **Unsplash**: High-quality free images
- **AI Generation**: Create custom images (DALL-E)

```
Number of images: 1-5
Image prompt: Based on your article topic
Size: Thumbnail, Medium, or Large
```

### Step 6: Generate Article Content

Set your parameters and generate:

```
Article length: 500, 1000, 1500, or 2000 words
Language: English (or your chosen language)
Creativity: Balanced (default)
```

The AI will:
- Write introduction and conclusion
- Develop each outline section with <h3> headings
- Maintain consistent tone and style
- Include proper formatting (paragraphs, lists, etc.)
- Integrate your selected images

### Step 7: Review and Edit

Review the generated article:
- Edit content directly in the editor
- Adjust formatting
- Add or remove sections
- Reposition images
- Save to your workbook

## Troubleshooting

### Keywords seem irrelevant to my topic

**Possible Causes**:
- Topic description too vague or broad
- Ambiguous terminology

**Solutions**:
- Be more specific with your topic
- Use descriptive phrases instead of single words
- Generate multiple sets and select best keywords
- Add your own keywords manually

---

### Generated titles are too long/short

**Possible Causes**:
- Length parameter not matching needs
- Topic complexity requires longer explanation

**Solutions**:
- Adjust maximum title length parameter
- Regenerate with different length setting
- Edit generated titles manually
- Create custom title using AI suggestions as inspiration

---

### Article outline doesn't match my needs

**Possible Causes**:
- Keywords not aligned with desired structure
- Outline generation based on generic patterns

**Solutions**:
- Modify the outline before generating article
- Add, remove, or reorder sections
- Be more specific with keywords
- Generate new outline with refined keywords

---

### Generated article is too short/long

**Possible Causes**:
- Word count parameter doesn't match expectations
- Complex topics may generate longer content

**Solutions**:
- Check word count setting before generation
- Regenerate with adjusted length parameter
- Edit content to desired length
- Split long articles into multiple parts

---

### Images don't match article topic

**Possible Causes**:
- Generic image prompts
- Limited relevant images in stock libraries

**Solutions**:
- Try different image source (Pexels vs Unsplash)
- Use AI generation for more specific images
- Manually select images after generation
- Refine topic keywords for better matching

---

### Article content is repetitive

**Possible Causes**:
- Outline sections too similar
- Keywords too closely related

**Solutions**:
- Diversify outline sections before generation
- Use broader range of keywords
- Edit outline to ensure distinct sections
- Regenerate with revised outline

---

### Lost progress / can't resume article

**Possible Causes**:
- Multiple incomplete articles in system
- Browser session cleared

**Solutions**:
- Check "My Articles" list for saved progress
- Complete or delete old incomplete articles
- Work through wizard in single session when possible
- Save explicitly at each step
