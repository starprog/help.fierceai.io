# Text Generation Tools

Master the art of AI-powered writing with FierceAI's text generation capabilities.

## Available Text Tools

### Article Writer
Generate comprehensive articles on any topic with proper structure and research-backed content.

**Features:**
- Multiple article lengths (500-5000 words)
- SEO-optimized content
- Fact-checked information
- Citation suggestions

**How to Use:**
1. Navigate to **AI Tools > Text Generator > Article Writer**
2. Enter your topic and requirements
3. Specify word count and tone
4. Click "Generate Article"

## Troubleshooting

### Content Quality Issues

**Problem**: Generic or low-quality output
**Solution**: Provide more specific prompts with examples

**Problem**: Incorrect tone or style
**Solution**: Use tone specifications and provide style examples

**Problem**: Factual inaccuracies
**Solution**: Always fact-check and provide source requirements in prompts

### Token Management

Monitor your usage to avoid running out of tokens:
- Use shorter prompts when possible
- Generate in sections for long content
- Preview before full generation
