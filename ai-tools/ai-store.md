2. View all available token packages

### Step 2: Select Package

Choose the package that best fits your needs based on:
- **Your Budget** - How much you want to spend

## Token Usage

### How Tokens Work

Different AI features consume different amounts of tokens:

- **Text Generation** - Charged per word or character
- **Image Generation** - Charged per image (typically 50+ tokens)
- **Audio Generation** - Charged per character or second
- **Video Generation** - Charged per frame or duration
- **Special Features** - Variable rates depending on complexity

> **Tip:** Check individual feature documentation for specific token rates.

### Token Allocation

Purchased tokens can be:
- **Automatically Allocated** - Distributed across all AI tools
- **Manually Allocated** - Assigned to specific features you use most
- **Shared (Team Plans)** - Available to all team members

### Token Balance

View your current token balance:
1. **Dashboard** - Main dashboard displays total tokens
2. **AI Store** - Balance shown when browsing packages
3. **Feature Pages** - Balance displayed when using AI tools

## Pricing & Value

### Understanding Value

Compare packages using:

**Tokens per Dollar Ratio**
- Higher ratio = better value
- Featured packages often offer best ratios
- Limited time offers typically beat regular pricing

**Example:**
```
Regular Package: 10,000 tokens @ $50 = 200 tokens/$
Limited Offer: 15,000 tokens @ $40 = 375 tokens/$ (87% better value!)
```

### Cost Optimization Tips

1. **Buy During Sales** - Wait for limited time offers when possible
2. **Larger Packages** - Often provide better per-token pricing
3. **Bonus Tokens** - Free extra tokens with select packages
4. **Email Alerts** - Subscribe to receive sale notifications
5. **Plan Ahead** - Stock up during major promotional periods

### Eligibility

Refunds may be available if:

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do tokens expire?**  
A: Yes, most packages have a validity period shown at purchase. Check your package details for specific expiration dates.

**Q: Can I share purchased tokens with team members?**  
A: If you have a Team plan, tokens can be shared with team members. Regular accounts are for individual use only.

**Q: What happens if a payment fails?**  
A: Payment failures prevent token delivery. Verify payment method details and try again. Contact support if issues persist.

**Q: Are there quantity limits on purchases?**  
A: Some packages may have per-user purchase limits. Check package details or contact support for enterprise needs.

**Q: How do I get notified about limited time offers?**  
A: Enable email notifications in your account settings to receive alerts about sales and promotions.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I don't use all my tokens?**  
A: No, tokens are non-refundable once credited to your account. Plan your purchases based on expected usage.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**  
A: No hidden fees. The price shown at checkout is the final amount you'll pay (subject to local taxes where applicable).

**Q: Do token packages include subscription benefits?**  
A: No, token packages are one-time purchases. For recurring benefits, consider subscription plans instead.
