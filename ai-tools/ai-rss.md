## How It Works

### Processing Flow

1. **Enter RSS Feed Title**
   - Copy a headline or title from any RSS feed
   - Paste into the AI RSS interface
   - Can be news headlines, blog post titles, or any feed item

2. **Configure Output Settings**
   - **Language:** Choose output language (100+ languages supported)
   - **Maximum Length:** Set desired word count (e.g., 500, 1000, 2000 words)
   - **Tone of Voice:** Professional, casual, informative, persuasive, etc.
   - **Creativity Level:** Adjust AI creativity (0.0-1.0 scale)
   - **Number of Results:** Generate multiple variations

3. **AI Blog Post Generation**
   - System creates structured blog post from title
   - Generates introduction, body paragraphs, and conclusion
   - Adds HTML h2 subheadings for proper formatting
   - Groups content logically with clear sections

4. **Real-Time Output**
   - Content streams in real-time as it's generated
   - See results immediately without waiting
   - Faster perceived performance

5. **Edit & Publish**
   - Review generated content in workbook
   - Edit inline or copy to clipboard
   - Publish directly to your blog or CMS
   - Save to documents for future use

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use this for any RSS feed?**  
A: Yes, the feature works with any RSS feed title or headline. You manually copy the title and paste it into the generator.

**Q: How accurate is the generated content?**  
A: AI generates content based on the title and general knowledge. Always fact-check and verify information before publishing, especially for news content.

**Q: Can I generate in multiple languages?**  
A: Yes, you can output in 100+ languages regardless of the original title language.

**Q: How long does generation take?**  
A: Content streams in real-time, typically 15-60 seconds depending on desired length and model selection.

**Q: Is the content unique?**  
A: Yes, AI generates original content for each request. Multiple generations from the same title will produce different articles.

**Q: Can I edit the generated content?**  
A: Absolutely! All content appears in your workbook where you can edit, refine, and customize before publishing.

**Q: What's the maximum article length?**  
A: You control length via the maximum_length parameter. Typical range is 500-5,000 words depending on your needs.

**Q: Does it include images?**  
A: No, this feature generates text content only. You can add images manually or use other AI image generation features.

**Q: Can I automate RSS-to-blog publishing?**  
A: The feature itself requires manual title input. For full automation, you'd need to integrate via API or use third-party automation tools.

**Q: Will Google penalize AI-generated content?**  
A: Google evaluates content quality, not generation method. Ensure content is accurate, valuable, and well-edited. Add unique insights and verify facts.
