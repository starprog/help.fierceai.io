## Best Practices

> **Tip**: Be specific in your prompts for better results!

### Writing Effective Prompts
- Be clear and specific about what you want
- Include context and background information
- Specify the tone and style you prefer
- Mention your target audience

### Example Prompt
Instead of: "Write a blog post about AI"

Try: "Write a 1000-word blog post about how AI is transforming small businesses, focusing on automation benefits, written in a professional but approachable tone for business owners."

## Token Usage

Each AI generation consumes tokens based on:
- **Input length** - Longer prompts use more tokens
- **Output length** - Longer responses cost more
- **Model complexity** - Advanced models cost more

Monitor your usage in the [dashboard](dashboard/overview.md) to stay within your plan limits.
